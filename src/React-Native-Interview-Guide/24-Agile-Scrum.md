# Agile & Scrum for Senior React Native Developers

---

# 1. What is Agile?

## Definition

Agile is a software development methodology that focuses on:

* Iterative Development
* Customer Collaboration
* Continuous Feedback
* Faster Delivery
* Adaptability to Change

---

## Agile Manifesto Values

### Individuals and Interactions

over

Processes and Tools

---

### Working Software

over

Comprehensive Documentation

---

### Customer Collaboration

over

Contract Negotiation

---

### Responding to Change

over

Following a Plan

---

## Interview Answer

"Agile is an iterative software development methodology focused on delivering value continuously while adapting to changing requirements."

---

# 2. Why Agile?

## Traditional Waterfall Problems

```text
Requirements
 ↓
Design
 ↓
Development
 ↓
Testing
 ↓
Release
```

Problems:

* Late Feedback
* Slow Delivery
* High Risk

---

## Agile Approach

```text
Plan
 ↓
Develop
 ↓
Test
 ↓
Release
 ↓
Feedback
```

Repeated frequently.

---

## Benefits

* Faster Releases
* Better Collaboration
* Continuous Improvement
* Reduced Risk

---

# 3. What is Scrum?

## Definition

Scrum is the most popular Agile framework.

It helps teams deliver software incrementally using short development cycles called Sprints.

---

## Scrum Framework

```text
Product Backlog
        ↓
Sprint Planning
        ↓
Sprint
        ↓
Daily Scrum
        ↓
Sprint Review
        ↓
Sprint Retrospective
```

---

## Interview Answer

"Scrum is an Agile framework that organizes work into time-boxed sprints with regular planning, review, and improvement activities."

---

# 4. Scrum Roles

## Product Owner

Responsible for:

* Product Vision
* Prioritization
* Backlog Management

---

## Scrum Master

Responsible for:

* Facilitating Scrum
* Removing Blockers
* Team Coaching

---

## Development Team

Responsible for:

* Designing
* Developing
* Testing
* Delivering Features

---

## Interview Answer

"The Product Owner prioritizes business needs, the Scrum Master facilitates the process, and the Development Team delivers working software."

---

# 12. User Story

## Definition

A feature described from the user's perspective.

---

## Format

```text
As a User

I want to Login

So that I can access my account
```

---

## Example

```text
As a customer

I want to save addresses

So that checkout becomes faster
```

# 20. Kanban

## Definition

Visual workflow management method.

---

## Flow

```text

 ↓
In Progress
 ↓

 ↓
Done
```

---

## Difference From Scrum

| Scrum          | Kanban          |
| -------------- | --------------- |
| Sprint Based   | Continuous Flow |
| Fixed Duration | No Sprint       |
| Velocity       | Cycle Time      |

---

# 21. Scrum vs Agile

| Agile               | Scrum                   |
| ------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Methodology         | Framework               |
| Broad Concept       | Specific Implementation |
| Multiple Frameworks | One Agile Framework     |

---

## Interview Answer

"Agile is the philosophy, while Scrum is one of the frameworks used to implement Agile."
